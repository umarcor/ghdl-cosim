6 Existing C headers

In this proposal, vhpi_user.h from opensource.ieee.org/vasg/Packages is used. However, inspecting other open source projects reveals that different versions of headers for VHPI and VPI are used.

  • Verialtor: verilator/verilator@master: include/vltstd
  • cocotb: cocotb/cocotb@master: cocotb/share/include
  • GHDL:
    • ghdl/ghdl@master: src/grt/vpi_user.h
    • ghdl/ghdl@master: src/grt/vpi_thunk.h
    • ghdl/ghdl@master: src/grt/vpi_thunk.c
